Melbourne Ice Membership Charter, Terms and Conditions

Introduction

The Melbourne Ice Limited

The Melbourne Ice is a semi-professional ice hockey team in the Australian Ice Hockey League [AIHL]

The team plays its home games at the O'Brien Group Arena [OBGA] in Melbourne, which was completed in 2010.

The Melbourne Ice are Australia’s pre-eminent Ice Hockey Team and have won the National Championship four times over the last 15 years winning a three-peat of premierships in 2010 – 2012 and then again in 2017.

In 2017 the club transformed into an unlisted public company and is now known as the The Melbourne Ice Limited.

Melbourne Ice offers fans and supporters the opportunity to purchase memberships on an annual basis which includes Club Membership, Discounted Seats, Discounted Merchandise and other exclusive benefits.

Membership Types

Melbourne Ice Limited offers four classification of Members:

Reserved Seat Memberships

Referred to as Reserved Seat Membership and does include the same numbered seat for the entire AIHL season.

Adult: Any person over the age of 18#

Concession & Youth (15-18): IHV Registered Player# Membership or Holder of a Statutory Concession Card.

Junior: Any child up to the age of 15# requiring a reserved seat or General Admission Seat. Babe in Arms: Any child under the age of three, held by the parents or guardian.

General Admission (STANDING Only)

Referred to as General Admission and does not include a dedicated or guaranteed seat in the stands.

Adult: Any person over the age of 18#

Concession & Youth (15-18): IHV Player Membership or Holder of a Statutory Concession Card.

Junior: Any child up to the age of 14# requiring a reserved seat or General Admission Seat.

Babe in Arms: Any child under the age of three, held by the parents or guardian.

Distance or Remote Supporters

This is non-ticketed distant membership which does not include game entry or a seat to any AIHL game throughout the season.

Life Members

Complimentary Memberships that will be offered by the club including a dedicated Numbered Seat.

Leadership Team & Volunteers

Subsidized Memberships including a Numbered Seat.

The club may at its discretion introduce a new or modified classification of Membership at any time during the year.

Membership Purchase

Memberships are only available to purchase online through the Melbourne Ice Community Portal. Payments are accepted by Credit Card, Pay Pal and also provide for a Payment Plan option.

If payment plan options are selected, the Payment Plan must be complete before the first of April every year.

If you are successful in your application for membership, the Club will send you an acceptance letter confirming your membership and membership benefits.

The Club reserves the right to not accept any memberships application at its absolute discretion.

Membership Terms

Upon the successful purchase for membership, Melbourne Ice will make available to the member fulfillment pack and membership card.

Member fulfillment packs and Membership cards will be available either by personal pick-up at a nominated Pre-season launch event or by direct mail.

Melbourne Ice reserves the right to not accept any application form at its absolute discretion and also reserves the right to suspend or cancel a membership without refund to any member that breaches these terms and conditions or, in the opinion of the Club’s board of directors, is guilty of conduct unbecoming of a member or prejudicial to the interests of the Club.

Memberships are not transferable and can ONLY be used for the person nominated on the Membership Card. Melbourne Ice reserve the right to not admit any person not carrying a dedicated named Membership Card.

The membership card remains the property of Melbourne Ice at all times. Membership cards may not be transferred, sold, exploited for commercial use, or used for promotional purposes or campaigns without the express written permission of Melbourne Ice Limited.

Membership Rights

Applicable Games

  • All home games at the Obrien Group Arena [April-September]
  • All Melbourne Ice Women’s Team Home games [October – March]

Non-Applicable Games

  • Pre-season or Post Season Tournaments [either home or away].
  • Exhibition Games.
  • Away Derbies where the Melbourne Mustangs have the ‘Home Game’.
  • All-star weekends.
  • Finals weekend.
  • Seats in the VIP or Corporate Hospitality areas throughout the rink.

Non-Transferable

Membership Cards and the associated numbered seats are not transferable for any games or for any reason.

Access to the Pre-Season & Jersey Presentation Event

Members will have free access to the annual pre-season and Jersey Presentation event.

Access to the Post Season Event

Members will receive free access to our post season wrap-up and celebration event.

Access to the Post Game Clubhouse

Members gave free access to the post game events social club with location of this post match function to be promoted through all membership communication. Members must present their 2017/18 membership card upon request and if you cannot produce your 2017/18 membership card, you will not be granted free admittance and will be requested to pay the entry fee.

Discount on Club Merchandise

Members will also receive 20% discount on all club merchandise purchased on the Melbourne Ice On-Line Store. Members will also be able to pick up these purchases at the next home game as long as the purchases take place by ‘close of business’ Tuesday prior to that home game. Purchases that are made after ‘close of business’ Tuesday prior to the next home game can elect to pick up at the next game or receive by surface mail at the standard delivery cost. This discount does not apply to Delivery or Handling charges.

Note: Discounts are not available to Limited edition ‘Fanware and Memorabillia” [including Pre-Worn Game Jerseys].

Meet and Skate with the Players

Members will have free access to at least one annual meet and skate with the players, leadership team and coaches.

Member Feedback Sessions

Members are entitled to attend Member Feedback sessions on a range of subjects throughout the year.

Lost Cards

The Melbourne Ice Ltd. is not responsible for membership cards that are lost, stolen or destroyed.

Lost membership cards must be reported immediately by emailing Member Services on XXXXXXXX and a replacement card will be issued within 10 business days. Please note that the charge of $20.00 per card will be applied. A temporary admission ticket will only be allowed if the Member has reported the lost card via the process above.

Admission to games will require an accepted form of identification.

Refunds

Refunds may only be requested for consideration up to the beginning of the Annual AIHL Season.

Any person seeking a refund must do so in writing, addressed to the Membership Manager @ memberships@melbourneice.com.au. By doing so, the member’s request will be taken in to consideration, however they are not guaranteed a refund. Members will need to return their core membership pack and membership card to the Club before a refund is processed. If approved a refund will be processed and made within 20 business days.

Special Needs

If a member has special needs, and holds a state issued Companion card, the member is eligible to receive a second membership, of the same category as they purchase, at no charge. This membership can then be used by a caretaker when attending ice hockey matches with that member. Note the Companion Card must reference the relevant Melbourne Ice member.

Members Code of Conduct

It is important that all spectators at a Melbourne Ice AIHL approved game are able to enjoy the game in a safe and comfortable environment;

Accordingly, each person present at a scheduled Melbourne Ice AIHL game must;

  • Respect the decisions of match officials
  • Respect the rights of each person present at a game
  • Not engage in the use of violence or intimidation at any time
  • Comply with the venue condition of entry
  • Conduct themselves in a manner and goodwill of the family nature of Melbourne Ice.
